About me

I do all kinds of stuff.

I can hear you from here: wow, I was right this instant looking for someone to do all kinds of stuff. Well, haven’t you found the right place.

I especially enjoy unconventional projects which constitute an intellectual, creative, or technical challenge. Even better if they also have a political reach.

For example

Video design for the stage, anticolonial and anticapitalist initiatives, weird and quirky video games, projects prioritising accessibility, interactive installations. I usually don’t take on corporate or advertising work.

Résumé

I’ve worked as a freelancer since 2000, studied graphic design at UQAM, and took part in the founding of cooperatives Molotov and Territoires, as well as collectives Foumalade and Grand bruit. I also cook a fine vegetarian poutine sauce.

Colophon

The source code for this website is available on GitHub. The text is set in Cogito by Production Type. I only use locally-sourced, organic, fair-trade pixels.
