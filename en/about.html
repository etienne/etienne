I do design and video and 3D. All kinds of stuff.

I can hear you from here: “wow, I was right this instant looking for someone to do all kinds of stuff”. Well, haven’t you found precisely the right place.

Examples of cool projects I would happily work on

  • Video design for the stage
  • Anticolonial and anticapitalist initiatives
  • Weird and quirky video games
  • Projects prioritising accessibility
  • Interactive installations

I especially enjoy unconventional projects which constitute an intellectual, creative, or technical challenge. Bonus points if they also have a political and progressive reach.

Please note that I don’t take on corporate or advertising work.

Résumé

I’ve worked as a freelancer for more than 15 years (one doesn’t get any younger), I studied graphic design at UQAM, and took part in the founding of cooperatives Molotov and Territoires, as well as collectives Foumalade and Grand bruit. I also cook a decent vegetarian poutine sauce.

Colophon
