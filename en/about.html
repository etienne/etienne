I can hear you from here: wow, I was just now looking for someone to do all kinds of stuff. Well, you've come to the right place.

I especially enjoy unconventional projects that pose an intellectual, creative, or technical challenge. Even better if they also have political reach.

Projects I like

Video design for the stage, anticolonial and anticapitalist initiatives, weird video games, projects prioritizing accessibility, interactive installations. I usually don’t take on corporate or advertising work.

Résumé

I’ve worked as a freelancer since 2000, studied graphic design at UQAM, and took part in the founding of the Molotov and Territoires cooperatives, as well as the Foumalade and Grand bruit collectives. I also make a fine vegetarian poutine sauce.

Colophon

The source code for this website is available on GitHub. The text is set in Cogito by Production Type. I only use locally-sourced, organic, fair-trade pixels.
