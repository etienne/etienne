My PROCJAM Proposal

My goal is to design a simple and flexible 2D isometric tile system.

Building on the style I developed for Apollo 11, I will focus on plain shapes, volumes, and colors — basic elements meant to be used and combined in creative ways.

While I intend to provide the typical staples of isometric tile systems — terrain, roads, walls — I would also like to explore less common themes: faces, insects, molecules. Fun!
