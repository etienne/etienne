Leda & St.Jacques had a clear vision of the dreamlike visual effects they wanted to conjure alongside the otherworldly set and costume design of their video Ariane: pulsating lights and swarms of ethereal matter. I collaborated closely with them to bring that vision to life.

Music : « Hôtel Amour » by Ariane Moffatt. Produced by Rodeo. Animation and compositing work done as part of the Foumalade collective.
