Leda & St.Jacques had a clear vision of the dreamlike visual effects they wanted to conjure alongside the otherworldly set and costume design of their video Ariane: pulsating lights and swarms of ethereal fireflies. I collaborated closely with them to bring that vision to life.

Produced by Rodeo. Animation and compositing work done as part of the Foumalade collective.
