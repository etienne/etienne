Ariane

Magic swarms of fireflies for a video by Leda & St.Jacques.

Leda & St.Jacques had a clear vision of the dreamlike visual effects they wanted to conjure alongside the otherworldly set and costume design. It involved pulsating lights and swarms of ethereal fireflies. I collaborated closely to help realize that vision.

Produced by Rodeo. Animation and compositing work done as part of the Foumalade collective.
