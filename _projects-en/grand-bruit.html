--- title: Grand Bruit description: A vocal and visual experience about the traces we leave behind. date: 2015-04-02 published: true type: design language: en layout: project tags: [Concept, Programming] --- {% include hero.html alt="A screenshot of Grand Bruit" %}

In this interactive installation, the human voice becomes traces that dither and fade away. It pays homage to the abandoned village of Grand Bruit, and to the fleeting memories of its former inhabitants.

Produced by the NFB and Arte as part of the Interactive Haikus project.
