Faust

Wings of gold for Mephistopheles.

In this teaser for the opera Faust, Mephistopheles had to break with classical representations of the character. Building on the tone and language of the costume design, I came up with these imposing golden wings.

In collaboration with Opéra de Montréal and Dee. Work done as part of the Foumalade collective.
